Privacy Policy

Last updated: 3 June 2026

Just Graphics ("we", "us", "our") operates the website just-graphics.art. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we protect it.

1. Data We Collect

We collect only the information you voluntarily provide through our contact forms:

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Car model (optional)

We also automatically collect anonymous usage data through cookies and tracking technologies (see Section 4).

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To respond to your enquiry and provide a quote
  • To contact you regarding our services
  • To improve our website and advertising effectiveness

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your contact information is stored in our CRM system (Bitrix24). We retain your data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described above, or as required by applicable law. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use the following technologies:

  • Meta (Facebook) Pixel — tracks page views, form submissions, and button clicks to measure advertising performance and deliver relevant ads. Meta may use this data in accordance with their Data Policy.
  • Meta Conversions API — sends event data (form submissions, WhatsApp clicks) server-side to Meta for improved ad measurement accuracy.

Data sent to Meta is hashed (SHA-256) before transmission where applicable. You can opt out of Meta's tracking via your Ad Preferences.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Cloudflare — website hosting and content delivery
  • Meta Platforms — advertising and analytics
  • WhatsApp — customer communication
  • Bitrix24 — customer relationship management

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications
  • Opt out of cookie-based tracking
